Biography

Dr. Priyadarshini Lingaraj

MS (OBG)Laparoscopic Surgeon Fetal MedicineMedical Educator Women's Wellness

Dr. Priyadarshini Lingaraj is a highly accomplished Specialist Gynaecologist and Obstetrician with over 12 years of rich clinical experience. Currently practising at NMC Speciality Hospital, Abu Dhabi, she has built a reputation for excellence in complex surgical procedures and compassionate patient care.

Her expertise spans the full spectrum of women's reproductive health — from routine gynaecological concerns to complex high-risk pregnancies and advanced laparoscopic surgeries. She has successfully performed over 1,000 laparoscopic surgeries and overseen more than 1,000 deliveries, including cases involving gestational diabetes, preeclampsia, and multiple pregnancies.

Dr. Priyadarshini is fluent in English, Tamil, Telugu, Hindi, and Malayalam — making her especially beloved among Abu Dhabi's diverse expatriate community, where hearing a familiar language transforms a clinical visit into a comforting experience.
